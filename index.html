Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how 9HeavenIdleSaga ("we," "our," or "us") handles information in relation to the mobile game "9Heaven Idle Saga" (the "Game"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Important: 9Heaven Idle Saga is a fully offline, single-player game. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or information from our users.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. The Game operates entirely offline and does not transmit any information from your device to external servers or third parties.

Specifically, we do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address, etc.)
  • Device identifiers (advertising IDs, device IDs, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or gameplay statistics
  • Payment information
  • Any other personal or sensitive information

Data Storage

All game data (including save files, game progress, and settings) is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

The Game uses local device storage to save your progress. This data is stored in the app's private directory and is not shared with other apps or services. If you uninstall the Game, this local data will be deleted along with the app.

Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services that collect data. The Game does not integrate with:

  • Analytics services (Firebase Analytics, Google Analytics, etc.)
  • Advertising networks (AdMob, Unity Ads, etc.)
  • Social media platforms
  • Cloud storage services
  • User authentication services
  • Any other third-party data processors

Internet Connectivity

The Game does not require an internet connection to function. It operates completely offline and does not request internet permissions. No data is transmitted over the internet at any time.

User Accounts

The Game does not require or support user accounts. There is no registration, login, or account creation system. All game progress is stored locally on your device.

Children's Privacy

While the Game is rated 18+ due to simulated gambling mechanics (gacha/summoning system), we do not knowingly collect any information from users of any age, as we do not collect information from anyone.
